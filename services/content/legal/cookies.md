Cookie Policy for Systemprompt.io
=================================

Effective Date: April 4, 2024 | Last Updated: April 4, 2024

1\. Introduction
----------------

This Cookie Policy explains how Systemprompt.io ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and only use essential cookies for the proper functioning of our service.

2\. What are Cookies?
---------------------

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide necessary information for the proper functioning of the site.

3\. Our Use of Cookies
----------------------

At Systemprompt.io, we have a minimal and privacy-focused approach to cookie usage. We want to emphasize that:

*   We do not use any cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.
*   We only use essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the functioning of our website.

The essential cookies we use are:

*   Authentication cookies: Used for secure sign-in functionality.
*   Session cookies: Used to maintain your logged-in state and secure your session.

These cookies are crucial for allowing you to log in to our service securely and maintain your session while using Systemprompt.io. They do not collect any personal information beyond what is necessary for these functions.

4\. Third-Party Cookies
-----------------------

The social sign-in functionality may set cookies from third-party providers (such as Google, Facebook, or other social media platforms you use to log in). These cookies are governed by the respective third-party's privacy policy and are only used for authentication purposes.

5\. Managing Cookies
--------------------

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting the ability to set cookies may affect the functionality of our website, particularly your ability to use the social sign-in feature.

6\. Changes to This Cookie Policy
---------------------------------

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7\. Contact Us
--------------

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at:

Email: [ed@systemprompt.io](mailto:ed@systemprompt.io)

We are committed to maintaining your privacy. Our use of cookies is limited to essential functions only, and we do not engage in any form of user tracking or data collection beyond what is strictly necessary for the operation of our service.
